Welcome to Provence - Home cooking is our speciality

Provence is a family run business. Established 2002. Provence is run by Lucy & Simon Higgins. Provence is situated in the heart of Malahide village, North county Dublin. We open early every morning right through until 6:00pm (9pm weekends). During the day, it is a great place to have breakfast, a delicious lunch or a coffee & scone. It is by far the most popular choice in Malahide for daytime dining. Much of our success is due to serving a wide range of delicious home-cooked food. We also have tasty blackboard specials daily for extra variety. Our restaurant has a capacity of approximately 50 people.
